<objective>
Complete the ingest path: a Queue consumer Worker dispatches each job to a Cloudflare Container running yt-dlp + ffmpeg, which performs the hybrid download, extracts the audio track and keyframes, parses metadata, stores artifacts in R2, and creates/updates the Finding in D1. The consumer marks status and notifies the user via Telegram on success or failure, leaning on Queue retries + dead-letter for resilience.

Purpose: Turns a queued submission into a durable, media-complete Finding — the end-to-end spine of the product.
Output: Container image + ingest logic, the Queue consumer, and a Telegram notify helper.
</objective>
